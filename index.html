Logic & Reasoning

A curated collection of classic puzzles in logic, probability, and game theory.

These puzzles are designed to be explored rather than simply solved. Each begins with the full scenario, then unfolds through a sequence of simpler perspectives before revealing the complete explanation and underlying principle.
